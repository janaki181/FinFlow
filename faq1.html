Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is FinFlow?

FinFlow is a comprehensive financial management platform designed to help individuals and businesses optimize their cash flow, track investments, and make informed financial decisions. Our suite of tools includes budgeting, investment tracking, ROI calculators, and financial forecasting.

Is FinFlow suitable for personal or business use?

FinFlow is designed for both personal and business use. We offer different plans tailored to individual needs, small businesses, and enterprise solutions. Each plan includes features specific to the financial management needs of these different user types.

How secure is my financial data on FinFlow?

Security is our top priority at FinFlow. We implement bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL), two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Your data is stored in secure, redundant servers, and we never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. Additionally, we are compliant with all major financial data protection regulations.

Account & Pricing

How much does FinFlow cost?

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing cycle. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you can downgrade to our free plan and continue using basic features without charge.

Features & Tools

What is the ROI Calculator and how does it work?

The ROI (Return on Investment) Calculator is a powerful tool that helps you evaluate the potential return on your investments. You input your initial investment amount, expected annual revenue, annual costs, and investment timeframe. The calculator then provides you with total return, net profit, ROI percentage, and payback period.

This tool is particularly useful for business planning, comparing investment opportunities, and setting realistic financial goals. While it provides estimates based on your inputs, actual returns may vary based on market conditions and other factors.

Can FinFlow connect to my bank accounts?

Yes, FinFlow can securely connect to over 10,000 financial institutions worldwide. This includes banks, credit cards, investment accounts, and loan providers. Our read-only connections ensure that your accounts remain secure, and you can manage which accounts are connected at any time through your dashboard.

This integration allows for automatic transaction tracking, real-time balance updates, and comprehensive financial reporting without manual data entry.

Does FinFlow offer financial advice?

FinFlow provides financial insights and analytics based on your data, but we do not offer personalized financial advice. Our tools can help identify trends, opportunities, and potential issues in your finances, but for specific investment or tax advice, we recommend consulting with a qualified financial advisor.

Business and Enterprise plans do include access to our financial analyst team for general guidance and tool optimization.
